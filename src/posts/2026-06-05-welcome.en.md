Metro CAT (Metro Machinery) is proud to launch a redesigned website, built to help customers reach equipment, parts and service information more easily than ever.

## What's new

- **Bilingual support** — Thai and English, switchable in a single click
- **Article hub** — knowledge and maintenance tips for Caterpillar equipment
- **Promotions page** — regularly updated special offers and campaigns

## What's next

We will continue adding content that helps your operation — case studies, preventive-maintenance guides and the latest Caterpillar product news.

Follow us so you never miss an update, and whenever you need advice on machines or parts, our team is always ready to help.
