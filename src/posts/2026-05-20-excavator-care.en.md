Caterpillar excavators are famous for their durability, but correct maintenance keeps them performing at their best and adds years of service life.

## 1. Check fluid levels daily
Engine oil, hydraulic oil and coolant are the lifeblood of your machine. Check levels and condition before every shift.

## 2. Clean the air filter regularly
Dust on construction and mining sites clogs filters quickly, affecting power and fuel consumption.

## 3. Care for the undercarriage
Inspect track tension and wear — the undercarriage accounts for the majority of an excavator's maintenance cost.

## 4. Always use genuine parts
Genuine Caterpillar parts are engineered to fit and last, reducing the risk of failure mid-job.

## 5. Plan preventive maintenance
Schedule periodic inspections with the Metro CAT service team to catch issues early and cut unplanned downtime.
